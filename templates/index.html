01. About Me 👋

Hello! My name is Paolo and I'm a die hard Bay Area sports fan, hardstuck diamond Valorant player, and recovering anime addict. My love-hate relationship with programming began back in 2018 when a couple of my friends and I joined a hackathon for fun and ended up creating a winning project with skills we learned that same day!
